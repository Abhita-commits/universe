This experience uses **Augmented Reality (AR)** to place a 3D model of our solar system right in your physical space using your device's camera.

How It Works & Safety:

  • **Desktop/3D View:** You can **zoom, pan, and rotate** the solar system using your mouse/touch.
  • **AR Activation:** On a compatible phone, an **"Enter AR"** button will appear after this screen. Click it!
  • **Security:** **AR REQUIRES HTTPS.** If AR fails, ensure you are viewing the page over **HTTPS** (e.g., your GitHub Pages link).
  • **Data Safety:** **No camera data is recorded, saved, or transmitted.**
