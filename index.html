This experience uses **Augmented Reality (AR)** to place a 3D model of our solar system right in your physical space using your device's camera.

How It Works & Safety:

  • **Desktop View:** You can **zoom, pan, and rotate** the solar system using your mouse until you click "Start AR".
  • **Camera Access:** We need camera permission to enable the AR view.
  • **Data Safety:** **No camera data is recorded, saved, or transmitted.** The feed is only used locally for the AR experience.
  • **Interaction (AR):** Look for a pulsating ring (**the reticle**) on a flat surface. Tap the screen to place the solar system!
  • **Safety:** Please ensure you are in a safe, open area while using AR.
